Data Type Profile: 314e Age

Official URL: http://314e.com/fhir/ig/StructureDefinition/age-314e Version: 1.0.0
Active as of 2026-06-18 Computable Name: Age314e

314e profile of the FHIR Age datatype.

Age is a constrained Quantity used to express a duration of elapsed time, typically of a human being as read from a clinical record.

Usages:

  • Use this DataType Profile: 314e AllergyIntolerance, 314e Condition, 314e Condition Encounter Diagnosis, 314e Condition Problems Health Concerns... Show 2 more, 314e Procedure and 314e RequestGroup
