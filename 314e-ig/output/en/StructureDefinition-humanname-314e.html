Data Type Profile: 314e HumanName

Official URL: http://314e.com/fhir/ig/StructureDefinition/humanname-314e Version: 1.0.0
Active as of 2026-06-18 Computable Name: HumanName314e

314e profile of the FHIR HumanName datatype.

This profile constrains the period element to use the 314e Period profile.

Usages:

  • Use this DataType Profile: 314e Organization, 314e Patient, Patient Mother's Name, 314e Practitioner and 314e RelatedPerson
